# MATTBEARCADE Focus

MATTBEARCADE is the modern arcade district of the MATTBEAR ecosystem.

It should not become a duplicate of RETROCADE.

## Role

- MATTBEARCADE = modern playable hub
- RETROCADE = classic preservation arcade
- Music toys = connected because pads, timing, rhythm, launchers, and browser audio overlap
- Examples = showcase/demo archive

## Design rule

Use the current ecosystem doctrine:

- obsidian black
- smoked chrome
- tungsten gold
- cyan system glow
- magenta media accents
- green live states
- amber active development
- red danger/arcade energy

The goal is underground luxury arcade, not rainbow seizure bunker.
